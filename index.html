Start your journey

Pigeon’s Map is an Android application idea that makes route planning easy and efficient for multiple stops. It is mainly designed for postal workers but it can be used by anyone who needs to go to multiple places with minimum time and effort.

Enter a list of addresses and their priorities and get the shortest path. This way, you will save time and energy.
